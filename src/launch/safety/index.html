The absence of gravity in space can cause a variety of problems for humans, including muscle and bone loss, and other physiological changes. It can also make it difficult to perform tasks and move around, as objects do not behave in the same way they do on Earth. Not to worry though because due to our patented Bone Restore technology rockets, humans will still be able to live as if they would be around gravity. Working along with Space X, we were able to devolop many solutions one being our specialzed regen suits built for recovery.


Space is filled with high-energy particles that can be harmful to humans. These particles can damage DNA and other cellular structures, and increase the risk of cancer and other diseases. Thanks to the Radionite fabric that covers the interior of our spacecraft, we can maintain safety standards that are within those of the CDC in terms of radiation. Kjell Norwood Lindgren a former astronaut on the ISS has partnered with lift to ensure the safety of all of all our passagengers by providing research with Space radiation exposure.
