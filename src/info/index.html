About us


A realm full of wonders beyond the imagination of others lies an exciting and mysterious place that is full of wonders and mysteries that we are only just beginning to understand. LIFT is a space travel company aiming to achieve an accessible and safe traveling experience to space. We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of human exploration and driving innovation in the field of space exploration.

Our team is committed to delivering the highest quality service to our customers and to expanding the frontiers of knowledge and understanding through the exploration of the universe. Through the tours and services we provide, we strive for affordable space travel for all.

Starting up

​​LIFT was founded in 1999 with the vision to harness space technology for international development while pursuing space science research and planetary exploration. Our company began with the advancement of space technology through the testing of rockets and space travel. We worked on perfecting a rocket model which would be able to effectively transport people to space.

Dr. Bartholemew Wishnowski, widely regarded as the father of Space tourism, is the founder and chairman of LIFT. He is a renowned scientist and a visionary leader who played a crucial role in establishing the foundation of the tourist space program. Under his guidance, LIFT made significant progress in the fields of satellite communications, meteorology, and remote sensing, among others. Dr. Wishnowski's contributions to this space company have had a lasting impact and continue to inspire future generations of space scientists and researchers. We are dedicated to fulfill his goal of making space accessible for everyone.
