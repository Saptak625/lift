24-day training regimen


Training step 1

All medical screening for each participant traveling must be completed during the first part of training. This medical screening will include different physical tests that ensure that the traveler is capable of flying safely without any complications. The list of tests that will be present during this stage will be a basic screening of physical attributes, including height, weight, vision, etc. Additionally, a fitness screening will also be conducted for each participant, testing their level of endurance, strength, and flexibility to make sure that they are capable of space travel. These different tests are necessary for the training as they make sure that each participant is capable of flying through space over a prolonged period of time.

Training step 2

The next step of space training will include basic space travel training.  Space tourists will need to receive basic training in the principles of space travel, including the effects of microgravity on the body and safety protocols. Participants must be prepared for the effects of space on the body. Additionally, the participants will go through mission-specific training in which they will receive special training for the particular mission or package they choose. Space tourists will need to know how to operate any equipment or perform any tasks that may be required during their space travel experience.

Training step 3

In the third stage of training, we will train the participants to be ready for space and its environment. This includes different simulations that will portray different possible scenarios in the case of an emergency.  It's important that space tourists know how to respond in the event of an emergency while in space. Training may include simulations of various emergency scenarios and drills to practice emergency procedures. This emergency training consists of different simulations that depict a real-like scene in which the tourist must be able to perform the correct duties in the case of an emergency.

Training step 4

In the last part of the training, the tourists will undergo more simulations that depict the gravity-less environment of space. This last part of the training will include a full review of the training program as well as a rehearsal of the actual launch. This will ensure that all the participants are ready for the launch on the specified date.
