Step 1 : Re-Entry


We understand that space travel can be a daunting experience, and that's why we've put together this recovery training to help you understand what to expect after coming back to Earth. We'll be with you through the process to ensure that you have a smooth and comfortable journey back to normalcy.


Preparation Technology Expert Guidance

Preparing for re-entry is essential to ensure that you are ready for the challenges ahead. This involves a comprehensive pre-entry training program that covers both the physical and emotional aspects of returning to Earth. The training includes exercises to help astronauts adapt to Earth's gravity, as well as psychological counseling to help them cope with the stress and emotional challenges of returning to their families and daily life on Earth.

The lift used for re-entry is equipped with advanced technology designed to make the transition smoother and more comfortable for astronauts. This includes body-support systems that help reduce the impact of the increased gravitational force on the body, as well as gravity simulation technology that provides a gradual transition from microgravity to Earth's gravity. The lift is also equipped with advanced life-support systems to ensure the safety and well-being of the astronauts during the re-entry process.

Throughout the re-entry process, a team of experts will be on hand to monitor the condition of the astronauts and provide guidance and support. This team includes medical professionals, psychologists, and other specialists who are trained to deal with the unique challenges of re-entry. They will closely monitor the physical and emotional condition of the astronauts and provide guidance and support to ensure a smooth and successful transition back to Earth.

Step 2: Medical evaluation


Upon re-entry, we will conduct a comprehensive medical examination of all of our passengers to ensure that you are capable and ready to proceed to the next steps of recovery. Our team of medical professionals will assess your physical and mental health, check for any injuries or illnesses, and develop a customized plan for your recovery. This may include meditation, physical therapy, and other treatments as needed. We will also provide further assistance to passengers facing complex medical problems upon recovery through our wide array of health professionals and services.

Rest and recovery are essential components after reentry from space travel. After the medical examination, you will be given time to rest and recuperate in our comfortable facilities that include access to a variety of food cuisines, relaxation activities such as yoga and meditation, and spacious living quarters designed for your comfort. Our dedicated team will provide you with the care and attention you need to make a full and speedy recovery.
