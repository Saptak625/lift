What can I do to keep myself safe?


Follow the instructions of the spacecraft crew and listen to any announcements that are made. The crew will be responsible for managing the spacecraft's systems and ensuring a safe return to Earth.

Stay seated and strapped in during the descent back to Earth. The spacecraft will experience high G-forces during re-entry, and it's important to remain in your seat to avoid injury.
