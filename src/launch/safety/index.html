Concerns and Dangers of Space Travel
Concern 1: Travel without Gravity

The absence of gravity in space can cause a variety of problems for humans, including muscle and bone loss, and other physiological changes. It can also make it difficult to perform tasks and move around, as objects do not behave in the same way they do on Earth. Not to worry though because due to our patented technology rockets, humans will still be able to live as if they would be around gravity.
